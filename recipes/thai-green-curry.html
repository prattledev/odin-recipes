Thai Green Curry

Home

Description

If you want a completely authentic recipe, and one that maximizes deliciousness, this is it right here. It is one of the best known dishes of Thai cuisine, and despite its complex flavours, it is actually quite easy to make at home. It's also gluten free, and adaptable for vegans!

Ingredients

  • Thai Basil
  • Bamboo Shoots
  • Red Bell Pepper
  • Green Curry Paste
  • Lime Leaves
  • Coconut Milk
  • Fish Sauce

Method

  1. Reduce the coconut milk until very thick
  2. Add the curry paste and fry until fragrant
  3. Add the chicken and cook until sealed
  4. Add the bamboo shoots, lime leaves, and fish sauce
  5. Add Chicken Stock
  6. Add Thai basil and stir just until wilted
  7. Season to taste and serve with rice
